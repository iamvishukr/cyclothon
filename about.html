About Us

Welcome to the official website of Cyclothon, organized by the esteemed i3 Foundation! A thrilling blend of adventure, fitness, and community spirit, Cyclothon is not just an event; it's a movement to inspire health, environmental awareness, and unity. Join us on this journey as we pedal towards a healthier and greener tomorrow.

Cyclothon is held every month on the 2nd Sunday, making it a recurring celebration of fitness and sustainability. Mark your calendars and be part of this ongoing movement!

About i3 Foundation

At the heart of Cyclothon lies the i3 Foundation, an organization dedicated to empowering communities, fostering sustainability, and promoting health and fitness. With a legacy of impactful initiatives and a commitment to creating positive change, i3 Foundation aims to transform lives and inspire action through events like Everest Summit, TEDxKanke, and Cyclothon.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to create a healthier world, one step—or pedal—at a time. We believe in the power of collective action, and Cyclothon embodies this spirit by uniting people from all walks of life to ride for a cause.

Cyclothon will continue to be held every month on the 2nd Sunday, ensuring a consistent opportunity to engage with the community and promote our shared goals.
